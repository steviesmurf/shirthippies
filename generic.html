About Us

Hello there! We are a group of vintage collectors and enthusiasts; composed of storytellers, artists, musicians, and etc. We love expressing our personalities through our wardrobes; specifically vintage fashion. Each garment to us, holds a special story. And our goal is to help share and preserve yours. We are a small, local business that focuses on archival and curation. We have saved thousands of garments from the landfill by offering our services to our local neighbors and surrounding communities.

By supporting us, you are actively combating against for-profit organizations and climate change. Big for-profit organizations such as Goodwill and Salvation Army are only interested in turning your donated goods into profit, and by doing so, they ignore the consequences of their actions. Such exporting used clothing to third world countries to the point that it destroys their local economy while implicitly contributing to climate change.

Our service is simple. You exchange your old t-shirts with us. We give you cash in-return. Then your old t-shirts gets processed. And together, we kept them out of the landfills! Please don't hestiate to reach out if you have any questions and concerns. We are happy to accommodate to most requests (within reeason of course). We drive all over the Bay Area, and can come to any location. All you have to do is contact us through the sidebar to the left, and we will respond as soon as possible!

(Additionally, we offer a small reward for any individuals that can connect us with old t-shirts. We are looking for anything from the 70s/80s/90s upto the early 2000s. Any condition, any size. Please refer to the sidebar on the left for our contact information.)
